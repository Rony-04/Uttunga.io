What is Uttunga?



Uttunga - The Nexus Of Young Minds is a student forum which focuses on holistic development of the entire school of CS and IT, Jain University. Our belief of Involve• Innovate• Inspire• is what makes the forum unique and none like other. The forum mainly focuses on Information Technology based events, Projects and has so much more to offer to the society.
Technical Team
From hosting exciting Hack-a-thons, Code-a-thons and many other Tech based events to website designing, Our Technical team assures everything goes fluently without a single hitch
Design Team
Be it making enticing posters or finalizing an abstract idea, The design team believes in Applying empathy to collaboration and co-designing that helps everyone feel included
Public Relations Team
The Public Relations Team makes sure the right lime light to an event. Coming up with Marketing strategies and helping the forum with sponsors and corporate relations are the team’s forte
Documentation Team
The Documentation team ensures the consistency of documents, on document usage, and document flow across the forum and its activities
Cultural Team
Be it dance, Be it vocals or be it music…The cultural team is open for forms of expression and is a great platform to showcase talent
Photography and Videography
The team ensures that all the events, activities are captured perfectly so that moments can be remembered
Organizing
There is no organization without Manpower…The team is the literal definition of Organizing.
